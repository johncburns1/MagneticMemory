Magnetic Disk Memory and the RAMAC 305

  • Launched us into the era of magnetic disks
  • IBM 305 RAMAC (1956, IBM) --> World's first hard disk drive
  • Allow real-time random acces to sizeable quantities of stored data
    • First time ever
  • Process
    • 50 24" in diameter metal platter coated with magnetic material
    • Platters spit at 1200 rpm
    • 2 metal access arms that can be dynamically controlled by a servo motor
      • This allows the head to move to any position on and write
        information by magnetizing magnetic coating on matter
      • Heads can also read the information
    • Could only store about 3.75 MB of info

    Processing Unit:

    The processing unit contains a magnetic drum, on which there are storage tracks for program instructions, arithmetic and logic, and information
    being processed. The processing unit also contains a 100-character magnetic core unit, that is used
    for all transfers of information, and the control circuits for the processing operations"
